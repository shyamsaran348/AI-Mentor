# Project Summary

**Project Name:** Smart Traffic Control Using AI
**Domain:** Artificial Intelligence (AI)

## Objective
To design an AI-based traffic control system capable of monitoring traffic conditions, analyzing vehicle density, and improving traffic flow while reducing delays and congestion.

## Problem Statement
Traffic congestion remains one of the most significant challenges faced by modern cities. Fixed-cycle traffic signal systems often fail to respond effectively to varying traffic conditions, leading to inefficient utilization of road infrastructure. Traffic authorities require intelligent solutions capable of monitoring traffic conditions, identifying congestion patterns, and supporting data-driven traffic management decisions.

## Expected Outcomes
* Functional AI-based traffic monitoring prototype.
* Vehicle detection and traffic analysis system.
* Interactive dashboard for traffic visualization.
* Traffic congestion reports and insights.
* Performance evaluation of AI models.
* GitHub repository containing source code and project resources.
