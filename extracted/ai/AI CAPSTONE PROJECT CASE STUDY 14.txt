AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 14
ESPORTS AI BOT FOR ADAPTIVE GAMEPLAY
 
INTRODUCTION
The gaming and esports industry has experienced tremendous growth over the past decade, attracting millions of players and spectators worldwide. Modern games increasingly rely on Artificial Intelligence to create engaging gameplay experiences, intelligent opponents, adaptive difficulty levels, and personalized interactions. Traditional game bots often follow predefined rules and scripted behaviors, making them predictable and less challenging for experienced players.
Artificial Intelligence enables game agents to learn from player actions, adapt strategies, and make intelligent decisions in dynamic gaming environments. This project focuses on exploring how AI can be used to develop adaptive esports bots capable of analyzing gameplay situations and improving performance through continuous learning and decision-making.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Many game bots rely on fixed strategies and scripted decision-making, resulting in repetitive and predictable gameplay experiences. As player skill levels vary significantly, there is a growing need for intelligent opponents capable of adapting their behavior based on gameplay conditions and player actions.
Game developers and researchers require AI systems that can analyze game states, learn from interactions, and dynamically adjust strategies to provide engaging and challenging gameplay experiences.
How can an AI-powered esports bot be developed to analyze gameplay situations, adapt strategies, and provide intelligent decision-making in competitive gaming environments?
