AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 5
REAL-TIME SENTIMENT ANALYSIS OF SOCIAL MEDIA
 
INTRODUCTION
Social media platforms have become one of the primary channels through which individuals express opinions, share experiences, and discuss current events. Organizations, governments, businesses, and researchers increasingly rely on social media data to understand public perception, monitor brand reputation, and identify emerging trends. However, the massive volume of content generated every day makes manual analysis impractical.
Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) enable automated analysis of large amounts of textual data, helping organizations identify sentiments, opinions, and emotional trends in real time. This project focuses on exploring how AI can be used to analyze social media content and provide meaningful insights into public sentiment.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Organizations often struggle to understand public opinion due to the vast amount of social media content generated daily. Manually reviewing posts, comments, and discussions is time-consuming and inefficient. Businesses and decision-makers require intelligent systems capable of analyzing large-scale textual data and identifying sentiment patterns quickly and accurately.
There is a growing need for AI-powered solutions that can process social media data, classify sentiments, and provide actionable insights based on public opinion trends.
How can an AI-based sentiment analysis system be developed to analyze social media content and identify public sentiment in an accurate and efficient manner?
SCOPE OF WORK
