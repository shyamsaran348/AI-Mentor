AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 9
AI-ENHANCED AUGMENTED REALITY FOR INTERACTIVE LEARNING
 
INTRODUCTION
The advancement of digital learning technologies has created new opportunities for making education more engaging, immersive, and interactive. Traditional learning methods often rely on static content such as textbooks, presentations, and videos, which may not fully capture the attention of learners or effectively demonstrate complex concepts. Augmented Reality (AR) enables digital content to be overlaid onto the real world, creating interactive learning experiences that enhance understanding and engagement.
Artificial Intelligence further enhances AR by enabling intelligent interactions such as object recognition, gesture detection, voice commands, and adaptive content delivery. This project focuses on exploring how AI and Augmented Reality can be combined to create interactive educational experiences that improve student engagement and learning effectiveness.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Many educational concepts, particularly in science, engineering, medicine, and technology, are difficult to visualize using traditional teaching methods. Students often struggle to understand abstract concepts when learning is limited to text-based or two-dimensional content.
Educational institutions require innovative learning solutions that can provide immersive and interactive experiences while adapting to user interactions and learning needs.
How can an AI-enhanced Augmented Reality system be developed to create interactive educational experiences that improve learner engagement, understanding, and participation?
