AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 13
PROJECTED REALITY WITH AI FOR SCIENCE SIMULATIONS
 
INTRODUCTION
Understanding scientific concepts often requires students to visualize complex systems, processes, and interactions that may not be easily demonstrated using traditional teaching methods. Topics such as astronomy, molecular structures, physics experiments, and biological processes can be difficult to comprehend through textbooks and static images alone. Modern educational technologies are increasingly being used to create immersive learning environments that enhance understanding and engagement.
Projected Reality combines digital projection technologies with Artificial Intelligence to create interactive educational experiences where users can engage with simulated environments through gestures, objects, or movements. This project focuses on exploring how AI can enhance projected reality systems to support science education through interactive and immersive simulations.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Many scientific concepts involve abstract processes that are challenging for students to visualize and understand using conventional learning resources. Traditional classroom demonstrations may be limited by cost, safety concerns, or the availability of physical equipment.
Educational institutions require innovative solutions that can provide interactive visual experiences, improve learner engagement, and support a deeper understanding of scientific concepts.
How can an AI-powered projected reality system be developed to create interactive science simulations that enhance student engagement, visualization, and learning outcomes?
