AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 8
AI TUTOR FOR PERSONALIZED LEARNING
 
INTRODUCTION
Education is rapidly evolving with the integration of digital technologies and intelligent learning systems. Traditional learning environments often follow a one-size-fits-all approach, where all students receive the same content and learning pace regardless of their individual strengths, weaknesses, and learning preferences. As a result, some students may struggle to keep up, while others may not be sufficiently challenged.
Artificial Intelligence has the potential to transform education by creating personalized learning experiences tailored to individual student needs. Intelligent tutoring systems can analyze student performance, identify learning gaps, provide adaptive recommendations, and deliver customized learning pathways. This project focuses on exploring how AI can be used to support personalized education and improve learning outcomes.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Students have diverse learning styles, abilities, and rates of understanding. Traditional educational methods often find it difficult to address the unique needs of every learner, resulting in inconsistent learning outcomes and reduced engagement.
Educational institutions require intelligent systems capable of monitoring student progress, identifying areas of improvement, and adapting learning content based on individual performance.
How can an AI-powered tutoring system be developed to personalize learning experiences, provide adaptive recommendations, and support student learning effectively?
