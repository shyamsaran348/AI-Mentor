AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 1
SMART TRAFFIC CONTROL USING AI
 
INTRODUCTION
Rapid urbanization and increasing vehicle ownership have led to severe traffic congestion in cities worldwide. Traditional traffic management systems rely on fixed-timing traffic signals that operate without considering real-time traffic conditions. As a result, vehicles often experience unnecessary delays, increased fuel consumption, higher emissions, and reduced road safety.
Artificial Intelligence has emerged as a promising solution for improving urban mobility through intelligent traffic monitoring and decision-making. By analyzing traffic patterns and vehicle density, AI systems can assist in optimizing traffic flow and reducing congestion. This project focuses on exploring how AI can be applied to create smarter and more adaptive traffic management systems.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Traffic congestion remains one of the most significant challenges faced by modern cities. Fixed-cycle traffic signal systems often fail to respond effectively to varying traffic conditions, leading to inefficient utilization of road infrastructure.
Traffic authorities require intelligent solutions capable of monitoring traffic conditions, identifying congestion patterns, and supporting data-driven traffic management decisions.
How can an AI-based traffic control system be designed to monitor traffic conditions, analyze vehicle density, and improve traffic flow while reducing delays and congestion?
