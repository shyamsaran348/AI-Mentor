AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 3
 PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE IN MANUFACTURING
 
INTRODUCTION
Modern manufacturing industries depend heavily on machinery and equipment to maintain productivity, product quality, and operational efficiency. Unexpected equipment failures can lead to production downtime, increased maintenance costs, missed delivery schedules, and significant financial losses. Traditional maintenance approaches, such as reactive maintenance and fixed-schedule servicing, often fail to detect issues before failures occur.
With the rise of Industry 4.0, Artificial Intelligence has become a valuable tool for monitoring equipment performance and identifying patterns that may indicate potential failures. By analyzing operational and sensor data, AI systems can assist organizations in predicting maintenance requirements and supporting proactive decision-making. This project explores how Artificial Intelligence can be applied to manufacturing environments to improve equipment monitoring, reduce unexpected breakdowns, and support efficient maintenance planning.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Manufacturing organizations frequently face challenges in maintaining equipment reliability and minimizing operational downtime. Traditional maintenance strategies often rely on fixed schedules or repairs after failures have already occurred, resulting in increased costs and reduced productivity.
Industries require intelligent systems capable of continuously analyzing equipment data, identifying abnormal behavior, and providing early indications of potential equipment failures before they impact production operations.
How can an AI-powered predictive maintenance system be developed to analyze equipment performance data, identify early warning signs of failure, and support proactive maintenance decision-making in manufacturing environments?
