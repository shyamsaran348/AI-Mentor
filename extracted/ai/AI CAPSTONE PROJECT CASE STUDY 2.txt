AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 2
AI-POWERED HEALTH DIAGNOSTIC ASSISTANT
 
INTRODUCTION
The healthcare sector generates vast amounts of patient data, including symptoms, medical histories, laboratory reports, and diagnostic records. However, access to timely medical consultation remains a challenge for many individuals, especially in remote and underserved regions. Patients often rely on self-assessment or online information, which may lead to confusion, misinformation, and delayed treatment.
Artificial Intelligence has emerged as a powerful technology capable of analyzing health-related data and identifying patterns that may assist in preliminary health assessment. By leveraging machine learning and data analytics, AI systems can support users by providing insights based on symptoms and historical healthcare information.
This project explores the application of Artificial Intelligence in healthcare by developing an intelligent assistant capable of analyzing user-provided health information and supporting preliminary decision-making processes.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Millions of individuals seek health-related information online before consulting medical professionals. However, the reliability of self-diagnosis is often limited, and patients may struggle to identify potential health conditions accurately based on symptoms alone.
Healthcare providers and patients require intelligent tools that can assist in organizing symptom information, identifying possible health risks, and supporting informed healthcare decisions while ensuring that professional medical consultation remains essential.
How can an AI-powered health diagnostic assistant be developed to analyze health-related information and provide meaningful preliminary insights that support healthcare awareness and decision-making?
