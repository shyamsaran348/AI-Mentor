AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 7
AI-DRIVEN ENERGY CONSUMPTION FORECASTING
 
INTRODUCTION
Energy consumption is increasing rapidly due to population growth, urbanization, industrial expansion, and the growing use of electrical devices. Efficient energy management has become a critical challenge for households, industries, and utility providers. Accurate forecasting of energy demand can help organizations optimize resource allocation, reduce operational costs, improve grid stability, and promote sustainable energy usage.
Artificial Intelligence has the potential to analyze historical energy consumption patterns and identify trends that can support more accurate forecasting. This project focuses on exploring how AI can be used to predict future energy consumption and assist in making informed energy management decisions.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Energy providers and consumers often face difficulties in predicting future energy demand accurately. Unexpected fluctuations in consumption can lead to energy wastage, increased operational costs, and challenges in maintaining efficient power distribution.
There is a need for intelligent systems capable of analyzing historical energy data, identifying consumption patterns, and generating reliable forecasts to support better energy planning and management.
How can an AI-powered energy forecasting system be developed to analyze consumption patterns and predict future energy usage effectively?
