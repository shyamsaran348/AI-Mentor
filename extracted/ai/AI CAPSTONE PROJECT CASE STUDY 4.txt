AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 4
 AI-BASED RESUME SCREENING SYSTEM
 
INTRODUCTION
Recruitment is a critical process for organizations seeking qualified talent in an increasingly competitive job market. As companies receive hundreds or even thousands of applications for a single position, manually reviewing resumes becomes a time-consuming and resource-intensive task. Human recruiters often face challenges in identifying suitable candidates efficiently while ensuring consistency and fairness throughout the screening process.
Artificial Intelligence has the potential to streamline recruitment by automatically analyzing resumes, extracting relevant information, comparing candidate profiles against job requirements, and assisting recruiters in identifying suitable applicants. Intelligent screening systems can improve efficiency, reduce manual effort, and support data-driven hiring decisions.
This project explores the application of Artificial Intelligence in recruitment and talent acquisition by developing a system capable of analyzing resumes and assisting in candidate evaluation.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Organizations often receive a large volume of job applications, making it difficult for recruiters to manually evaluate every candidate efficiently. Traditional screening methods may lead to increased hiring time, inconsistent evaluations, and difficulties in identifying the most suitable applicants.
Recruitment teams require intelligent systems capable of processing resume information, identifying relevant qualifications, and supporting candidate shortlisting based on predefined criteria.
How can an AI-powered resume screening system be developed to analyze candidate profiles, evaluate their suitability for specific roles, and assist recruiters in making informed hiring decisions?
