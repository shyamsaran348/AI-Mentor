AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 12
 AI-POWERED INDUSTRY 4.0 VISION SYSTEM
 
INTRODUCTION
The emergence of Industry 4.0 has transformed traditional manufacturing by integrating intelligent technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, automation, robotics, cloud computing, and data analytics into industrial processes. Modern factories increasingly rely on smart systems capable of monitoring production activities, ensuring product quality, reducing human intervention, and improving operational efficiency.
Computer Vision is one of the most impactful technologies within Industry 4.0, enabling machines to interpret and analyze visual information from cameras and sensors. AI-powered vision systems can assist in product inspection, defect detection, object classification, inventory monitoring, and production line automation. These systems help manufacturers improve consistency, reduce waste, and support data-driven decision-making
This project explores the development of an intelligent vision system capable of analyzing industrial products and supporting quality control and automation processes within a smart manufacturing environment.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Manufacturing industries often face challenges related to product quality inspection, defect identification, component classification, and production monitoring. Traditional manual inspection processes can be time-consuming, labor-intensive, and susceptible to human errors, especially in high-volume production environments.
Organizations require intelligent systems capable of analyzing visual information in real time, identifying quality issues, and supporting automated manufacturing operations while improving efficiency and accuracy.
How can an AI-powered vision system be developed to analyze industrial products, identify defects or variations, and support intelligent decision-making in Industry 4.0 manufacturing environments?
