AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 15
GENERATIVE AI MODEL FOR IMAGE OR TEXT CREATION
 
INTRODUCTION
Generative Artificial Intelligence has emerged as one of the most transformative areas of modern AI, enabling machines to create new content such as text, images, audio, videos, and code. Unlike traditional AI systems that focus on classification or prediction, Generative AI models learn patterns from large datasets and generate original outputs that resemble human-created content. These technologies are increasingly being used in industries such as education, marketing, entertainment, design, healthcare, and software development.
Recent advancements in Large Language Models (LLMs), Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), Variational Autoencoders (VAEs), and Diffusion Models have significantly expanded the capabilities of Generative AI. This project focuses on exploring how generative models can be used to create meaningful and creative content while considering ethical, responsible, and practical applications.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Content creation often requires significant time, effort, and expertise. Organizations and individuals frequently need assistance in generating creative materials such as written content, images, educational resources, marketing assets, and digital designs. Traditional methods may not always scale efficiently to meet increasing content demands.
There is a growing need for intelligent systems capable of generating high-quality content while maintaining relevance, creativity, and consistency. Such systems should also consider ethical concerns, fairness, and responsible AI practices.
How can a Generative AI system be developed to create useful and meaningful content while ensuring quality, creativity, and responsible AI usage?
