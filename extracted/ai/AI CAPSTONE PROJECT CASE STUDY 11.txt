AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 11
AI NAVIGATION SYSTEM FOR SELF-DRIVING CARS
 
INTRODUCTION
Autonomous vehicles represent one of the most transformative applications of Artificial Intelligence in modern transportation. Self-driving systems aim to improve road safety, reduce traffic congestion, enhance mobility, and support efficient transportation networks. These systems rely on intelligent decision-making, environmental perception, and navigation capabilities to operate without constant human intervention.
Artificial Intelligence enables vehicles to analyze surroundings, identify obstacles, plan routes, and make driving decisions based on real-time data. This project focuses on exploring how AI can be applied to autonomous navigation systems and how intelligent vehicles can safely navigate dynamic environments.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Road transportation involves complex and constantly changing environments where drivers must continuously make decisions based on traffic conditions, road layouts, pedestrians, and other vehicles. Human errors remain one of the leading causes of road accidents worldwide.
There is a growing need for intelligent navigation systems capable of perceiving their surroundings, planning safe routes, and making informed driving decisions while adapting to changing road conditions.
How can an AI-powered navigation system be developed to enable autonomous vehicles to perceive their environment, plan routes, and navigate safely in dynamic driving scenarios?
