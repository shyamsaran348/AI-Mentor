AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 6
AI-POWERED CROP DISEASE DETECTION
 
INTRODUCTION
Agriculture plays a critical role in ensuring food security and supporting economies worldwide. One of the major challenges faced by farmers is the timely identification and treatment of crop diseases. Plant diseases can significantly reduce crop yield, affect product quality, and result in substantial economic losses. Traditional disease identification often requires expert knowledge, making early diagnosis difficult for many farmers, particularly in remote regions.
Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision technologies offer opportunities to automate crop health monitoring by analyzing plant images and identifying disease symptoms. This project focuses on exploring how AI can assist farmers in detecting crop diseases and supporting informed agricultural decision-making.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Crop diseases continue to impact agricultural productivity and profitability across the world. Farmers often face difficulties in identifying diseases at an early stage, leading to delayed treatment and increased crop damage. Access to agricultural experts may also be limited in certain regions.
There is a growing need for intelligent systems capable of analyzing crop images, identifying disease symptoms, and assisting farmers in monitoring plant health more effectively.
How can an AI-powered crop disease detection system be developed to identify plant diseases from images and support timely agricultural interventions?
