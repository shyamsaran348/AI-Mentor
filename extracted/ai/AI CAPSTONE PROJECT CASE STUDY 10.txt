AI CAPSTONE PROJECT 10
FACE RECOGNITION FOR ROBOT INTERACTION
 
INTRODUCTION
Humanoid robots and intelligent robotic systems are increasingly being used in education, healthcare, customer service, hospitality, and industrial environments. For robots to interact effectively with humans, they must be capable of recognizing individuals and responding appropriately. Face recognition technology enables robots to identify users, personalize interactions, and improve user engagement by providing context-aware responses.
Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision play a significant role in enabling robots to detect, recognize, and track human faces in real time. This project focuses on exploring how AI-powered face recognition can be integrated into robotic systems to create intelligent and personalized human-robot interactions.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Human-robot interaction becomes more effective when robots can recognize individuals and adapt their responses accordingly. Traditional robotic systems often provide the same interaction experience to every user, limiting personalization and engagement.
There is a need for intelligent robotic systems capable of identifying individuals, recognizing returning users, and supporting personalized interactions in various environments such as classrooms, offices, hospitals, and customer service centers.
How can an AI-powered face recognition system be developed to enable robots to identify users and support intelligent, personalized human-robot interactions?
